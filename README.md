# üç≤ To Supply Leftover Food to Poor (Salesforce Project)

## üìñ Overview
The **To Supply Leftover Food to Poor** project is a Salesforce-based cloud application developed to reduce food wastage and address hunger by connecting food donors, NGOs, and volunteers on one unified digital platform.  
It enables restaurants, hotels, and event organizers to donate surplus food efficiently, while NGOs and volunteers can coordinate pickups and deliveries in real time.

The platform promotes **sustainability, accountability, and transparency** in food redistribution through cloud automation and centralized data management.

---

## üéØ Objectives
- Minimize food wastage through structured digital food redistribution.  
- Connect donors, NGOs, and volunteers via a real-time cloud platform.  
- Enable efficient donation tracking and delivery verification.  
- Enhance transparency and trust between all stakeholders.  
- Support Sustainable Development Goals: **Zero Hunger** and **Responsible Consumption**.

---

## ‚öôÔ∏è Key Features
- **Donor Management:** Register donors (restaurants, hotels, etc.) and their food offerings.  
- **Food Listing System:** Create, view, and update available food donations instantly.  
- **NGO Coordination:** Allow NGOs to claim and schedule food pickups.  
- **Volunteer Tracking:** Assign volunteers for transportation and confirm delivery status.  
- **Real-Time Notifications:** Automatic alerts for new donations and delivery updates.  
- **Reporting Dashboard:** Display statistics for donations, beneficiaries, and impact.

## üöÄ Project Workflow
1. **Donor** registers and lists surplus food.  
2. **NGO** receives an automatic notification and claims the donation.  
3. **Volunteer** is assigned for pickup and delivery.  
4. **Delivery confirmation** is updated in Salesforce.  
5. **Reports** track total donations, deliveries, and beneficiaries served.
